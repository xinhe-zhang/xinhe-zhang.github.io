ABOUT HAZEL CHANG




Hazel was born and raised in Taichung, Taiwan. She studied industrial design as her undergrduate studies from 2016 to 2020. Hazel is equiped with knoweldge of multiple design research method and has gone through projects of versatile range -- from home appliances to user interface, from consumer eletronics to social welfare system. As a young designer she does not limit herself from dipping her toes into different fileds and is eager to find the crosspoints between human behavior, latest technology and refreshing ideas. Aside from product design, she is also interested in sustainabiliy, ecology, culture and history.
